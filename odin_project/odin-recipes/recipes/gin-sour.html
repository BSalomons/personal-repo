Gin Sour

Description

The Gin Sour is a nice smooth cocktail to introduce you to the night.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4oz Lime Juice
  • 3/4oz Simple Syrup
  • 3/4oz Egg White
  • 2oz Epitaph Gin

Steps:

  1. Combine all Ingredients in a Shaker with Ice
  2. Shake for 30 seconds
  3. Pour into flute glass and let settle
  4. Enjoy!
