London Mule

Description

The London Mule is a delicious cocktail made with Epitaph Gin and Ginger Beer.

Ingredients:

  • 1oz Lime Juice
  • 6oz Fever Tree Ginger Beer
  • 2oz Epitaph Gin

Steps:

  1. Fill a glass up with ice
  2. Pour lime juice over the ice
  3. Top with Ginger Beer
  4. Layer with Epitaph Gin
  5. Enjoy!
